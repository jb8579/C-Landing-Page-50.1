1. Background

The lottodaily.co.nz website is owned and operated by Lotto New Zealend.
This Policy outlines how we collect, store, and use Personal Information, including the use of cookies. It also describes how you can access or update your Personal Information or raise concerns with us.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that all practices comply with applicable New Zealand privacy regulations.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Information refers to data that identifies an individual or makes their identity reasonably identifiable;

  • Cookies are small data files stored on your device to improve your experience and track usage;

  • Website refers to our domain, lottodaily.co.nz;

  • We, us, and our refer to Lotto New Zealend.

3. What Personal Information do we collect and hold?

This Policy applies to Personal Information. The type of information we collect depends on your interactions with our services. We may collect:

  • Name

  • Date of birth

  • Mailing address

  • Email address

  • Telephone number

  • Bank account details

  • Credit card information

  • Signature

  • ID documents for account verification

  • Login credentials

To prevent fraudulent activity, we may require users to verify their identity before purchasing lottery tickets. This is at our sole discretion.

4. Why and how we collect Personal Information

We may use your Personal Information for the following purposes:

  • To verify user identity;

  • To manage subscriptions and lottery services;

  • To process purchases and maintain user accounts;

  • To notify users of winnings or account updates;

  • To handle billing and payments securely;

  • To enhance and develop our digital services;

  • To inform users of new offers or products (users may opt-out);

  • To monitor for fraudulent or unauthorized activity;

  • To respond to support requests or feedback.

We may share your Personal Information with trusted third-party service providers to support the delivery of our services.
What are 'cookies'?

  • Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality and performance.

  • Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device through your web browser.

  • They help us remember your preferences and improve your shopping experience.

  • Disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features or complete purchases on our website.

5. Use and disclosure of Personal information

We use Personal Information to manage lottery services, deliver winnings, and improve user experiences on lottodaily.co.nz.

6. Personal Information Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your Personal Information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Important Notice: cross-border disclosures

By interacting with our platform, you agree that your Personal Information may be stored or processed in countries outside New Zealand.

8. Access to Personal Information

You have the right to access or correct your Personal Information. To do so, log into your account at lottodaily.co.nz or contact us directly.

9. Enquiries and Complaints

If you have questions or concerns about how we manage your Personal Information, please contact our support team.

10. Updates to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
